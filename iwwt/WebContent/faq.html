Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for e-Ration?

To register for e-Ration, visit our Sign Up page and follow the instructions to create an account.

What documents are required for registration?

You will need a valid government ID, proof of address, and a recent photograph for registration.

How do I update my personal information?

After logging in, go to your Profile page, where you can edit your personal information.
